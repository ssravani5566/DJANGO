ECOMMERCE

E-commerce, or electronic commerce, refers to the buying and selling of goods and services over
the internet, encompassing a wide range of transactions and business models. Definition and Significance E-commerce involves commercial activities conducted online, allowing consumers and businesses to engage in transactions without the need for physical presence. It has transformed how people shop and how businesses operate, making it a crucial component of the global economy.
E-commerce is projected to reach significant sales figures, with estimates suggesting
it could surpass $6 trillion by 2026
